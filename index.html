Get started in minutes

01

Register

Create a namespace for your organization. Takes under a minute.

02

Publish

Push your registry data — public keys, membership lists, or revocation records.

03

Verify

Anyone can verify your records instantly via API. No manual checks needed.

terminal
# Register your org
$ dedi register --namespace your-org

# Publish a registry
$ dedi publish --type membership \
  --file registry.json

# Verify a record
$ dedi verify --namespace your-org \
  --record <id>

DeDi.global (Decentralized Directory) is infrastructure that transforms traditional public registries into cryptographically secure, instantly verifiable directories. Think of it as a universal trust layer for any public information your organization needs to share.

Unlike traditional databases, DeDi provides cryptographic proof of authenticity, decentralized availability (no single point of failure), unified discoverability and automatic trust propagation between recognized organizations.
