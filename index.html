What DeDi publishes

Four categories of
verifiable registries.

Governance · Organizations

Every member, verified on-chain.

The Problem

Organizations maintain member lists in spreadsheets, PDFs, or proprietary databases. Verifying membership requires manual checks, phone calls, or accessing siloed systems that may be outdated.

With DeDi

Members are published to a cryptographically signed registry. Any party can verify membership in milliseconds via a single API call. Updates propagate in real-time.

Cryptography · Identity

Trust anchors, globally discoverable.

The Problem

Public keys are exchanged bilaterally, stored in incompatible formats, and impossible to revoke reliably. There is no universal directory to discover an organization's current public key.

With DeDi

Organizations publish their public keys to a decentralized directory. Any party can discover and verify keys without bilateral exchange. Rotations and revocations are instant.

Compliance · Regulation

Regulations your systems can read.

The Problem

Regulatory policies exist as PDF documents, legal text, and website pages. Compliance teams manually interpret rules and translate them into system configurations. Updates lag by weeks or months.

With DeDi

Policies are published in machine-readable formats with cryptographic signatures. Systems can automatically discover, parse, and apply regulatory requirements. Changes propagate instantly.

Security · Trust

Revocations that arrive in real-time.

The Problem

Credential revocations are published on CRL endpoints, proprietary APIs, or not at all. Relying parties poll multiple sources on different schedules. Stale data creates security gaps.

With DeDi

Revocations are published to a unified registry with real-time push notifications. Subscribe once, get instant alerts across all issuers. No more polling, no more gaps.

Platform capabilities
Everything you need to publish & verify.
A complete API for managing the full lifecycle of public registries — from creation to real-time monitoring.
01
Publish & organize
Create namespaces and registries, publish records individually or bulk-upload thousands via CSV. Draft, review, then go live.
/create-namespace · /publish-records · /bulk-upload
02
Instant lookup
Resolve any namespace, registry, or record with a single API call. Advanced search across nested fields with filtering and pagination.
/lookup · /query · /search
03
Cryptographic verification
Verify the authenticity of any lookup result with cryptographic proof. Every verification is logged in a tamper-proof audit trail.
/verify-record-lookup · /verification-logs
04
Real-time watch
Subscribe to changes on any resource — namespace, registry, or record. Get push notifications the moment anything is updated or revoked.
/subscribe · /unsubscribe · /subscriptions
05
Delegation & access
Add delegates at the namespace or registry level for collaborative management. Verify domain ownership via DNS TXT records.
/add-delegate · /verify-domain
06
Lifecycle control
Suspend, reinstate, or permanently revoke any registry or record. Full version history with immutable audit trails and CSV export.
/suspend · /reinstate · /revoke · /versions
